> Main findings

• Standard design flow rate for a bathroom sink faucet in Finland is 0.1 L/s (6.0 L/min).
• Basin taps without a flow-limiting device should not exceed 6.0 L/min.
• Basin taps with a flow-limiting device may allow a maximum flow of 8.0 L/min, but the default water-saving setting must not exceed 6.0 L/min.
