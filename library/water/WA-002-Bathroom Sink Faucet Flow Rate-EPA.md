> Main findings

• Standard design flow rate for a bathroom sink faucet in Finland is 0.1 L/s (6.0 L/min).
• Modern water-saving faucets typically operate between 4.0 and 6.0 L/min.
• Older faucets may reach 8–12 L/min.
• EPA WaterSense certified faucets are limited to 5.7 L/min or less.
